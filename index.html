The Science Behind the Savings

Real Math, Not AI Guessing

ProPilot doesn't just use AI to "guess" at savings. It employs proven mathematical algorithms used by Fortune 500 procurement teams, then enhances the results with AI-powered contextual insights.

Every savings calculation is backed by statistical analysis, not wishful thinking. This is why our ROI predictions are consistently accurate.

  • Fuzzy String Matching
    Detects duplicate vendors even with spelling variations
  • Statistical Outlier Detection
    Identifies price anomalies using IQR method
  • Levenshtein Distance
    Measures similarity between vendor names mathematically
  • AI Context Layer
    OpenAI GPT-4 provides human-readable explanations
